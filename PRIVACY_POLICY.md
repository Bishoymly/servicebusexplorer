# Privacy Policy for Azure Service Bus Explorer

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Azure Service Bus Explorer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our desktop application.

## Information We Collect

### Connection Information
- **Azure Service Bus Connection Strings**: Stored locally on your device
- **Azure AD Credentials**: Used for authentication, stored locally
- **Connection Names**: Custom names you assign to connections

### Usage Data
- **Connection Usage**: Which connections you use and when
- **App Preferences**: Your application settings and preferences

## How We Use Your Information

- **To Connect to Azure Service Bus**: Connection strings and credentials are used solely to establish connections to your Azure Service Bus namespaces
- **To Store Preferences**: Your app settings are stored locally to provide a personalized experience
- **To Improve the App**: We do not collect analytics or usage statistics

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All connection strings, credentials, and preferences are stored **locally on your device**
- Data is stored using your operating system's standard storage mechanisms
- **We do not transmit any data to external servers**

### Data Security
- Connection strings and credentials are stored in your device's local storage
- We do not have access to your stored credentials
- All connections are made directly from your device to Azure Service Bus

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.**

- No data is sent to our servers
- No data is shared with analytics services
- No data is shared with advertising networks
- All connections are direct between your device and Azure Service Bus

## Third-Party Services

### Azure Service Bus
- When you connect to Azure Service Bus, your connection is made directly to Microsoft's Azure infrastructure
- We do not intercept or store messages or data transmitted through Azure Service Bus
- Your use of Azure Service Bus is subject to Microsoft's privacy policy and terms of service

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You can view all stored connections and data within the app
- **Deletion**: You can delete connections and data at any time through the app interface
- **Export**: Connection data can be managed through the app's interface

## Data Retention

- Connection data is retained on your device until you delete it
- Deleting a connection removes all associated data from your device
- We do not retain any data after you delete it from the app

## Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
